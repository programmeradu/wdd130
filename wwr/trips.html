Featured Adventures

Half-Day Splash

Fast and fun intro to whitewater. Great for families and first-timers. 3–4 hours on water.

  • Difficulty: Class II–III
  • Includes: raft, PFD, helmet
  • Snacks and water provided

Full-Day Classic

Our most-loved run: blend of splashy rapids and scenic floats. 6–8 hours on water.

  • Difficulty: Class III
  • Includes: raft, PFD, helmet, dry bag
  • Riverside lunch included

Big Water Challenge

High-adrenaline water for experienced paddlers. Must meet fitness and swim guidelines.

  • Difficulty: Class III–IV
  • Includes: raft, PFD, helmet, splash top
  • Pro guide and safety briefing
